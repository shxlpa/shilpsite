4 Aug 2024

Love Notes

I spent my summer springing between work in Torrance and the Sierra Nevada mountain range. Engineering as an artist is tricky; it feels unemotional and inhuman. Trapped in numbers and performance reviews, you start to wonder where we went wrong as a species. The mountains were my respite this summer. They provoked curiosity and art out of me each time I went. It was like filling up at a gas station at the start of each week.
